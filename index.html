SUSSEX UNIVERSE

Sussex Universe is the free public lecture series from the School of Mathematical and Physical Sciences at the University of Sussex. The lectures cover topics including astronomy, quantum technology, experimental and theoretical particle physics, mathematics, and materials science.

NOTE: Normally lectures are held at the University however during the current Covid-19 pandemic lectures will be exclusively online for the foreseable future. Lectures will go online on our YouTube channel at the usual time of 7pm, and there will be a live Q&A after the talk with the speaker, via Zoom when the lecture finishes (around 7.30pm) - links for both will appear below 15 minutes before the talks are scheduled to start.
